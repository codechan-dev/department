About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science and Business Systems is established with an intake of 60 in the year 2020. The Department offers a 4 years of B.Tech program in Computer Science and Business Systems to promote excellence in producing industry perspective professionals. This course is preferred by TCS and has been approved by AICTE to provide industry-ready professionals who add ethics and human values to the organization in the era of Business 4.0.
The students are also exposed to emerging topics such as Analytics, Deep learning, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things and Design thinking to enable them for industry ready employability.
